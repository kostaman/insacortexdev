E/S utilisées:

PA1:     Potar
PA0-PA2-PA3: Timer 2 channel 1, 3 et 4 -> Driver de PMOS (A, B, C)
PA4-PA5: ADC4 et ADC5 -> Acquisition de BFEM_A et B
PA9-PA10: UART1

PB6-PB7: Timer 4 channel 1 à 2 -> Encodeur
PB8-PB15: Leds

PC0-PC3: Ecran LCD
PC7-PC9: Timer 3 channel 2 à 4 -> Driver de NMOS (A, B, C)
PC4-PC5: ADC14 et ADC15 -> Acquisition de BFEM_C et V_BAT
PC13:    Bouton Tamp
PC10-PC12: Ecran LCD (controle)
